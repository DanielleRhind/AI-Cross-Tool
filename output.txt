3 Description
The TPS62850x is a family of pin-to-pin 1A, 2A 
(continuous), and 3A (peak) high efficiency, easy-
to-use synchronous step-down DC/DC converters. 
The devices are based on a peak current mode 
control topology. Low resistive switches allow up to 
2A continuous output current and 3A peak current. 
The switching frequency is externally adjustable from 
1.8MHz to 4MHz and can also be synchronized to 
an external clock in the same frequency range. In 
PWM and PFM mode, the TPS62850x automatically 
enters power save mode at light loads to maintain 
high efficiency across the whole load range. The 
TPS62850x provides a 1% output voltage accuracy 
in PWM mode, which helps design a power supply 
with high output voltage accuracy, fulfilling tight supply 
voltage requirements of digital processors and FPGA.
The TPS62850x is available in an 8-pin 1.60mm × 
2.10mm SOT583 package.
